## The Problem
The $28B U.S. notarization industry still runs on an ink stamp. Result: **$1.2B+ annual real estate notary fraud**, records that vanish when notaries retire, and identity that's verified exactly once on documents with 20-year horizons. The legacy industry's answer is "trust the platform." Ours is **"trust the math."**

## The Solution — Three Bets No Incumbent Has Combined
1. **AI Document Forensics** — GPT-5.2 Vision scans every document pre-seal, detecting tampering, font mismatches, copy-paste seals, and collisions with prior seals on-chain.
2. **Hedera Public Blockchain** — Every seal anchored to Hedera Consensus Service mainnet (topic `0.0.10373605`). Globally verifiable forever. Sub-cent per transaction. If NotaryChain disappears, the proof remains.
3. **Living Identity** — Continuous biometric re-attestation. Identity drift triggers real-time WebSocket alerts. Identity isn't a one-shot check — it's a continuous score.
