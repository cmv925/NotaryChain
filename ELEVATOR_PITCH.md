## Delivery Tips

### Pacing
- **Beats per second:** ~2.5 words. The scripts above are calibrated for this rate.
- **Pause for the punchline:** After the last line, count two beats silently before any follow-up.
- **Vary your tone on key proof points:** Slightly slower on numbers ($500K, $3.5M ARR, 7x), slightly faster on lists (the "three architectural bets").

### Key phrases to land
| Phrase | Why it works |
|---|---|
| "The proof outlasts the paper" | Visceral. Replays in the listener's head later. |
| "We're not asking investors to fund engineering risk" | Reframes the entire conversation. Most pitches lead with "we're going to build X." We lead with "we already built X." |
| "Don't trust NotaryChain — verify it yourself" | The most counter-intuitive trust positioning in the market. Memorable. |
| "Nine separate revenue streams" | Beats single-product startups. Investors hear "diversification = less risk." |

### Anti-patterns to avoid
- ❌ Don't say "We've built a blockchain platform" — sounds like every crypto startup. Say "We anchor proof to a public blockchain so it outlasts us."
- ❌ Don't say "We use AI" — meaningless. Say "GPT-5 Vision catches forgeries before a human sees the document."
- ❌ Don't mention specific competitors by name in the first 90 seconds — looks defensive.
- ❌ Don't ask "Does this make sense?" mid-pitch — assume yes; let them ask.

### After the pitch
**The investor's first question reveals their lens.** Common patterns:

| If they ask... | They care about... | Pivot to... |
|---|---|---|
| "What's your CAC?" | Unit economics | Section 6 of `FUNDING_PROPOSAL.md` — Trust Badge LTV/CAC 4.2x, SDK 11x |
| "Who's the team?" | Execution risk | Founder credentials + the 2 engineering hires being made |
| "Why won't [DocuSign/Proof] just copy this?" | Defensibility | The public-verifier moat + 2+ year Hedera replication runway |
| "How do you make money?" | Business model | Nine surfaces — but lead with the top 3 (notarization, Trust Badge, SDK) |
| "What's the regulatory risk?" | Compliance | FL RONSP-filed, multi-state evaluator fail-closed, 47 of 50 states legal |
| "How big can this be?" | TAM | $28B notarization, $7-10B RON by 2030, comparable: McAfee SECURE ($1.5B/yr) |

We're raising 500K — all of it going to two specialist engineers — to take a fully-built platform to market. Base case: 12 million ARR in three years."*
