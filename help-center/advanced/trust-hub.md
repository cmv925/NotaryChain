# Trust Hub

**Verify once on NotaryChain. Prove your identity to banks, real-estate portals, and partner platforms — without ever re-doing KYC.**

## What it is
A federated identity dashboard. Shows your active verifications, your TrustLayer attestations (Ed25519-signed proofs anchored to Hedera), and your connections to partner platforms that consume those proofs.

## Why it matters
KYC is expensive and annoying. With Trust Hub, your one NotaryChain verification becomes a portable credential — accepted by every federation partner.
