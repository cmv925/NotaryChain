# Biometric Passport

**Your facial geometry hashed and anchored to Hedera as a non-transferable NFT. Carry it across platforms forever.**

## What it is
A cryptographic identity primitive. Your biometric template is hashed (not stored) and the hash is minted as a non-transferable NFT on Hedera Token Service (HTS). Future verifications compare a live capture against the hash — no centralized biometric database to leak.

## Why it's better
- Privacy: only the hash lives on-chain
- Portability: you own the NFT, not us
- Fraud resistance: forging the NFT requires breaking Ed25519
