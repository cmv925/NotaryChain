# Compliance Vault (PCV)

**Documents that monitor themselves. Expirations don't surprise you. Audits don't require a fire drill.**

## What it is
The Predictive Compliance Vault — continuous integrity monitoring of every document in your vault. It tracks expiration, jurisdictional risk, beneficiary validity, and generates SOC2/ISO evidence packages on demand.

## What it does proactively
- Surfaces expiring documents via the **What's Next?** card
- Flags jurisdictional risk (e.g. a POA executed in FL being used in CA)
- Generates audit evidence with timestamps and cryptographic proofs
