# Ceremony Mode

**Turn a transactional notarization into a memorable event. Real-estate brokers and M&A attorneys upsell their clients on it.**

## What it is
A polished, branded variant of the standard RON ceremony with custom branding, music intro/outro, ceremony script, and a beautifully formatted commemorative PDF.

## When to use it
- Real estate closings (broker upsells)
- M&A signing celebrations
- Family-wealth transfer signings
- Adoption finalizations

## Why it matters commercially
Brokers and attorneys are paid to make their clients feel like the transaction is special. Ceremony Mode is the tool that lets them deliver it without producing it themselves.
