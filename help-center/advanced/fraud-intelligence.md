# Fraud Intelligence

**Network-wide AI that sees fraud patterns before they hit individual ceremonies.**

## What it catches
- Repeat fraudulent IDs across multiple attempts
- Location anomalies (signer claiming Florida but VPN-detected in Eastern Europe)
- Behavioral red flags from prior NotaryChain sessions
- Document re-use patterns indicating identity recycling

## Why network detection wins
Most RON fraud is patterned — the same bad actor tries multiple times. Single-ceremony anti-fraud misses the pattern. Fraud Intelligence sees the whole graph.
