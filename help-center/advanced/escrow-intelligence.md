# Escrow Intelligence

**Real smart-contract escrow on Hedera. Funds release only when the notarization is sealed and verified. Neither party has to trust the other.**

## What it is
Smart contracts deployed on Hedera HSCS (Hedera Smart Contract Service) holding funds in escrow until programmatic conditions are met — typically: the notarization is sealed AND identity verification is complete AND any signer-side conditions are satisfied.

## When to use it
- Real estate purchase deposits
- M&A signing-bonus releases
- High-value affidavits with conditional payouts
- Cross-border transactions where neither party trusts the other's local court system
