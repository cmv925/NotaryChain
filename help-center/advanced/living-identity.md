# Living Identity

**Not a one-time photo. Continuous biometric trust throughout every session.**

## What it is
Ongoing micro-verifications during notarization sessions. Traditional KYC checks ID once and assumes the same person is signing — Living Identity verifies continuously.

## What it catches
The most common form of RON fraud: a verified person hands the camera to an unverified accomplice mid-ceremony. Living Identity flags the handoff in seconds.
