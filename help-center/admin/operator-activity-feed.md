# Operator Activity Feed

**A live, color-coded stream of every action across NotaryChain. Compliance gold.**

## What it is
A real-time merged feed of in-memory (last 200) and server-side events: fetches, approvals, rejections, notary assignments, tour starts/completions, telemetry events. Each row shows surface (Command / Assurance / Client / Tour), action, actor email, outcome, and relative timestamp.

## Pro tip
Filter by failed outcomes during incident reviews. Patterns of failures often predict outages 24–48 hours in advance.

## SOC2 use case
You can answer "who approved this notary application and when?" in 3 seconds — for SOC2, audits, or forensics. Most platforms can't.
