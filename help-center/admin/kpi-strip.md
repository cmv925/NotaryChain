# Live KPI Strip

**Five real-time stat cards at the top of the Command Authority Suite. WebSocket-driven. No refresh needed.**

## What it shows
- Total Users
- Active Notaries
- Total Notarizations
- Revenue (USD)
- Pending Notary Applications

## Pro tip
Watch the **Pending Apps** card during business hours. Slow approvals = lost supply = lost revenue.
