# Notaries Management

**Your supply-side onboarding pipeline. Approve qualified notaries fast.**

## How to use it
1. Filter by **Pending** to see applications awaiting review
2. Click any to see credential photos, commission number, state, specialties
3. Approve or reject with one click — applicant is notified instantly

## Pro tip
Watch the **Pending** count on the Overview tab and aim for sub-24-hour approval turnaround. Faster supply = faster client experience = more revenue.
