# NotaryChain Power-User Cheat Sheet

> 30 tips. One page. The shortest path to mastering NotaryChain.

## For everyday signers

1. **Use Quick Seal for evidence, not legal acts.** Sealing a manuscript before a publisher reads it, a design before a copycat ships, a price quote before a vendor changes it — that's where Quick Seal earns its keep.

2. **Always run AI Summarizer before a notary call.** Understanding the contract beforehand saves you from a panic mid-ceremony — and notaries can't give legal advice.

3. **Run Doc Compare on every "minor revision."** Attorneys' "minor" sometimes means "we slipped in a clause that changes the deal." 4 seconds, free.

4. **Bulk Notarization beats 5 single sessions.** Real estate closings, estate planning, corporate filings — 50 documents in one 30-minute call.

5. **Add a recurring "annual estate review" reminder for January 1st.** 15 minutes of housekeeping per year prevents six-figure messes later.

6. **Put your crypto recovery phrases in Asset Vault.** Wrap them in a sealed envelope-style entry with beneficiaries. The #1 thing financial planners ask for and nobody does.

7. **Use a Verify link in your email signature.** It builds trust without any effort. Counter-parties can confirm your most recent notarized document is real in 5 seconds.

8. **Filter "Sealed on chain" in My Documents.** Only blockchain-anchored docs have shareable verification links — that's the filter you want when sending evidence.

9. **Pre-label documents in upload order for Bulk Notarization.** The notary walks through them in the order you uploaded.

10. **If your state has no notaries in Marketplace, screenshot the "Apply to join" CTA and send to your accountant.** They earn $25/notarization — and you might unlock your own state.

## For commissioned notaries

11. **Run AI Co-pilot before you click Start.** It pre-loads risk scores, signer red flags, and a 5-item ceremony checklist. You walk in looking like a 20-year veteran.

12. **Slow down on HIGH-risk Co-pilot flags.** Most fraud is caught here — not on camera.

13. **Let Co-pilot auto-fill your journal entry.** Click Generate, review, save. Statutory compliance in 2 seconds.

14. **Export your Notary Journal monthly as a SOC2 habit.** Lawyers and audit teams love this. Most notaries never do it.

15. **Print your Digital Seal public-key fingerprint on your business card.** It's a credibility flex other notaries don't have.

16. **Filter the Available queue by document type during slow hours.** Real estate closings pay more; routine POAs are faster.

17. **Mark 30-minute buffer slots between bookings.** Live notarizations sometimes run over — protect your schedule.

18. **Aim for sub-5-minute response times.** Statistically correlates with 4.8+ star ratings on the platform.

19. **Use Ceremony Mode for real-estate and M&A closings.** Brokers and attorneys upsell their clients on the polished experience — and tip notaries who deliver it.

20. **Set Schedule blocks for vacations a quarter ahead.** Clients can't book what isn't visible.

## For platform admins

21. **Watch the Pending Apps card during business hours.** Slow approvals = lost supply = lost revenue.

22. **Configure Slack alerts on Hedera latency > 2s.** Catches outages before customers complain.

23. **Export the Security & Compliance PDF monthly.** Build the habit before customers demand it. Procurement teams unblock faster when you can hand them a report on the spot.

24. **A/B test onboarding tour copy and watch Tour Completion rate.** If it drops below 50%, your onboarding is broken — and you'll know within hours.

25. **Disable accounts, don't delete them.** Status changes are audit-logged. Deletion isn't.

26. **Set high-severity Oracle Watchlist alerts for FL + TX.** Those two states drive 60% of US RON volume — you can't afford to miss a rule update.

27. **Filter Operator Activity Feed by failed outcomes during incident reviews.** Patterns of failures often predict outages 24-48 hours out.

28. **Build a one-paragraph "Compliance Statement" using the auto-generated Security PDF.** Reuse on every enterprise sales call.

29. **Restart Tour on demand from the user dropdown.** Best feature for live sales demos — the platform demos itself.

30. **Treat the Operator Activity Feed as your incident timeline.** When something goes wrong, it's already a chronological audit log — you just hand it to the auditor.
