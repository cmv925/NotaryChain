# Available Queue

**Every notarization request waiting for a notary in your state — sorted by urgency. Claim what you want, when you want.**

## What it is
A real-time job board. Clients submit RON requests; qualified notaries see them appear in the queue with Overdue / Urgent / Today priority badges. First to accept wins.

## How to use it
1. Open the **Available** tab on your Assurance Portal dashboard.
2. Review priorities — Overdue and Urgent get top placement.
3. Click **Accept** on the one you want.

## Pro tip
Filter by document type during slow hours. Real estate closings pay more; routine POAs are faster. Mix volume and value.

## FAQ
**How fast do I need to claim?** Average claim time on the platform is under 90 seconds for high-priority work.

**Can I un-claim a request?** Yes, with a one-click release — but it impacts your response-time score.
