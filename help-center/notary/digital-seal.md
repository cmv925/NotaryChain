# Digital Seal

**Your cryptographic embosser. Signed with Ed25519, anchored on Hedera, impossible to forge.**

## What it is
A unique cryptographic key pair that signs every notarization you perform. The public-key fingerprint is permanently anchored to Hedera, so any document you seal can be verified against your identity — by anyone, anywhere, forever.

## How to set it up
You configure it once during onboarding. Every notarization automatically uses it after that.

## Pro tip
Print your public-key fingerprint on your business card. It's a credibility flex that other notaries don't have — and verifiers love it.

## FAQ
**What if my key is compromised?** Rotate it from your profile. Old documents remain verifiable; new ones use the new key.

**Is this legally recognized?** Yes — Ed25519 signatures are accepted as electronic seals under the ESIGN and UETA acts and most state e-notary statutes.
