# AI Co-pilot — The Killer Feature for Notaries

**Walk into every ceremony looking like a 20-year veteran. AI pre-briefs you on the document, the signer, the jurisdiction, and the gotchas.**

## What it is
An AI assistant that reads the document, analyzes signer information, and produces a pre-ceremony briefing including: document-type classification, risk level (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH), key highlights, inconsistency flags, jurisdiction-specific compliance checks, and a 5-item ceremony checklist.

## How to use Co-pilot in 4 steps
1. Open any request → click **Analyze Request**.
2. Review the summary, risk level, and flags.
3. Optionally click **Generate** to auto-fill your journal entry.
4. Start the session armed with everything you need.

## Pro tip
Slow down on HIGH-risk flags. Most fraud is caught here — not in the camera. The Co-pilot is the difference between "I run notarizations" and "I run notarizations *well*."

## What Co-pilot detects
- Mismatched names between ID and document
- Dates that don't add up (signer too young, future-dated documents)
- Missing required clauses for the jurisdiction
- Unusual financial figures
- Signer behavioral red flags from prior NotaryChain sessions
