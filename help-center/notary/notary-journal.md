# Notary Journal

**Your statutory logbook. Auto-filled by AI. Blockchain-anchored. Exportable to court-ready PDF.**

## What it is
The statutorily required logbook every commissioned notary must maintain — modernized. Each notarization gets an automatic entry with signer name, ID type, document description, date, fee, and notes.

## How to use it
The AI Co-pilot generates the entry. You review, click **Save**. Done.

## Pro tip
Export your journal monthly as a SOC2 / audit-readiness habit. Most notaries never do — making yours stand out when an attorney asks.

## What gets exported
- Full chronological entries
- SHA-256 hashes of each notarized document
- Hedera transaction IDs (so any auditor can verify on-chain)
- A signed compliance attestation
