# Schedule & Availability

**Set your weekly hours, block vacation dates. Clients can only book slots when you're actually free.**

## What it is
Your availability calendar. Acts as the booking source-of-truth for the Marketplace — eliminating phone tag.

## How to use it
1. Open **Schedule** tab.
2. Drag your weekly availability windows.
3. Block specific dates (vacations, court appearances).
4. Save.

## Pro tip
Mark 30-minute **buffer** slots between bookings. Live notarizations sometimes run over — protect your schedule from cascading delays.
