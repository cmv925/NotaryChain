# AI Summarizer

**Upload a contract. Get the gist, the numbers, the dates, and the gotchas — in 90 seconds.**

## What it is
An AI-powered contract reader that compresses long legal documents into a digestible briefing: 5-bullet plain-English summary, every dollar amount, every deadline, every party named, and a risk-flag paragraph noting unusual clauses.

## When to use it
- Before signing any contract you didn't draft
- Before a notarization ceremony (so you actually know what you're signing)
- When opposing counsel sends you a 40-page agreement at 4:55pm
- Reviewing leases, NDAs, employment agreements, vendor contracts

## How to use it in 3 steps
1. Click **AI Summarizer**.
2. Upload your PDF.
3. Read the summary — usually 200–400 words.

## Pro tip
Always summarize a contract *before* the notary session. The notary can't give legal advice, but understanding the contract beforehand saves you from a panic mid-ceremony.

## FAQ
**Does it replace a lawyer?** No. It replaces the "I'll read it later" lie. For high-value contracts, still consult an attorney — but you'll arrive with better questions.

**What file types?** PDF, DOCX, images of contracts, even scans.
