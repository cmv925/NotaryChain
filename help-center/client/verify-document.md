# Verify a Document

**Paste a link or drop a sealed PDF. Confirm in 5 seconds: who signed it, when, in what state, and whether the seal is still valid. No login required.**

## What it is
A public verification tool. Anyone in the world — your counter-party, a bank, a court clerk — can confirm a NotaryChain-sealed document is authentic without contacting you or us.

## How to verify a document in 2 steps
1. Open `/verify`.
2. Either paste a verification link OR drop the sealed PDF.

## What you see
- Signer name and ID-verification method
- Timestamp (anchored to Hedera mainnet)
- State of notarization
- Notary commission number
- Whether the document hash matches the on-chain seal

## Pro tip
Put your verification link in your email signature, on your business website, or in your investor deck. It's effortless trust-building — verifiers don't have to take your word for anything.

## FAQ
**Do I need an account to verify?** No. Verification is public.

**What if someone alters the PDF?** Verification will fail — the hash won't match the on-chain seal.
