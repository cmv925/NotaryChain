# AI Document Generator

**Type what you need in plain English. AI drafts the legal document. 90 seconds.**

## What it is
A natural-language drafting tool. You describe what you need ("a power of attorney for my mother to manage my apartment while I'm overseas"), the AI produces a properly-structured legal document.

## When to use it
- Standard documents: POA, simple wills, NDAs, leases, deeds, demand letters, affidavits
- First drafts you'll hand to an attorney for review
- Urgent forms when you can't reach legal counsel

## How to use it in 3 steps
1. Click **AI Doc Generator**.
2. Describe what you need in 1–3 sentences.
3. AI drafts; you review, edit, and either save as a draft or send straight to a notary.

## Pro tip
For complex IP, M&A, or litigation documents, generate a first draft and hand it to an attorney. You'll cut their billable time by 60% because the structure is already correct.

## FAQ
**Is the output legal advice?** No. AI Document Generator produces drafts. For high-stakes matters, have an attorney review.

**Can I customize templates?** Yes — every draft is fully editable before saving.
