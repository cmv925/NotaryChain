# Quick Seal

**Drag and drop any file. We hash it, timestamp it on the Hedera blockchain, and give you back a verification receipt — in about 8 seconds.**

## What is Quick Seal?
Quick Seal is the fastest way to create cryptographic proof that a document existed in its exact form, at an exact second in time. It is **not** a wet-ink notarization — it's evidence. Think of it as a tamper-proof timestamp anyone in the world can verify.

## When to use Quick Seal
- Proving a manuscript existed before a publisher saw it
- Proving a design was yours before a copycat appeared
- Proving you sent a specific price quote at a specific moment
- Sealing meeting minutes, board resolutions, or contracts you don't need notarized but want timestamped
- Capturing evidence for IP disputes or litigation discovery

## How to use Quick Seal in 3 steps
1. Open `/dashboard` and click **Quick Seal** under Core Actions.
2. Drag your file (PDF, image, contract, anything) onto the upload zone.
3. Click **Seal Document**. You receive a verification link in seconds.

## What you get
- A cryptographic SHA-256 hash of your document
- A Hedera Hashgraph timestamp anchored on mainnet
- A public verification URL anyone can use without an account
- The original file remains private — only the hash is published

## Pro tip
Quick Seal is for evidence, not legal acts. For anything requiring a notary's stamp (wills, deeds, POAs), use [Request Notarization](./request-notarization.md) instead.

## FAQ
**Is Quick Seal legally binding?** It's evidence of existence and timing, accepted in most jurisdictions as proof that a document existed at a specific moment. It is not a substitute for notarization.

**Can someone see my document on the blockchain?** No. Only the SHA-256 hash is published — your original file stays private and encrypted in your vault.

**How long does the seal last?** Forever. Hedera is an immutable public ledger.
