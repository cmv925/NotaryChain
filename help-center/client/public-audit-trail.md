# Public Audit Trail

**A live ledger of every notarization on NotaryChain. Anonymous. Auditable. Trust-free.**

## What it is
A public, real-time stream of notarization events anchored to Hedera Hashgraph. No personal data is exposed — only document hashes, timestamps, and notary commission numbers. Anyone can verify our compliance and volume claims without our cooperation.

## Who uses it
- **Banks and insurers** auditing platform integrity
- **Investors** verifying platform volume claims
- **Regulators** spot-checking notarization counts
- **Skeptical counter-parties** confirming the platform is real

## How to use it
Open `/audit-trail` in a new tab. Watch the ledger tick. Each entry is clickable for the on-chain transaction.

## Pro tip
This is your investor-deck slide. "We don't ask you to trust us — the blockchain proves us." Most RON competitors can't show anything like this.

## FAQ
**Can someone see who I am?** No. The audit trail shows only hashes and timestamps. Your identity stays private.

**Why does this matter?** It's the same trust model as Bitcoin — verifiable without trusting the operator.
