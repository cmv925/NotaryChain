# Bulk Notarization

**Notarize up to 50 documents in one 30-minute ceremony — instead of running 50 separate sessions.**

## What is Bulk Notarization?
A single Remote Online Notarization ceremony where the notary walks you through a stack of documents in sequence. Designed for transactions that involve many forms — real estate closings, estate plans, corporate formation packets.

## When to use it
- Real estate purchase, sale, or refinance closings (often 20–40 docs)
- Estate planning packages (will, trust, POA, healthcare directive, beneficiary forms)
- Corporate formation (bylaws, operating agreements, capital tables, resolutions)
- Loan packages
- Adoption or guardianship packets

## How to use Bulk Notarization in 4 steps
1. Click **Bulk Notarization** under Core Actions.
2. Upload up to 50 PDFs.
3. Pre-label documents in the order you want to sign them — the notary walks through them in upload order.
4. Schedule a 30-minute slot with your notary.

## Why it's faster
- Single ID verification covers the whole stack
- Single video session, single Hedera anchor
- Single audit packet with all documents indexed
- Notary fee is bundled, not per-document

## Pro tip
Pre-label your documents in upload order. The notary will go through them in the order you uploaded — that's how the ceremony flows. A misordered stack adds 5–10 minutes of confused page-flipping.

## FAQ
**What's the maximum?** 50 documents per session. For larger packets, split into 2 sessions with the same notary.

**Are fees lower per-document?** Yes — typical bulk pricing is $10–$15 per signature versus $25–$50 standalone.

**Can different signers sign different documents?** Use [Multi-Signature](../advanced/multi-signature.md) for ceremonies with multiple signers.
