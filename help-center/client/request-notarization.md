# Request Notarization

**Meet a commissioned notary on video. Verify your ID. Sign. Receive a legally binding, blockchain-sealed PDF — without leaving your desk.**

## What is Request Notarization?
A full Remote Online Notarization (RON) ceremony. It's the exact same legal act as visiting a notary at a UPS Store, performed over secure video, with cryptographic identity verification and a blockchain-anchored seal. Legally binding in every state that recognizes RON.

## When to use it
- Powers of attorney, wills, trusts, codicils
- Real estate deeds, leases, mortgage documents
- Affidavits, declarations, sworn statements
- Loan documents and refinance packets
- International documents requiring apostille

## How to request a notarization in 5 steps
1. Click **Request Notarization** under Core Actions on `/dashboard`.
2. Upload your document.
3. Pick a notary from the [Marketplace](./find-notaries.md) — or let us auto-match by state and specialty.
4. Verify your identity (driver's license + LexisNexis InstantID quiz).
5. Join the scheduled video session, sign electronically, and receive your sealed PDF + blockchain receipt.

## What makes our RON different
- Every ceremony is recorded and stored encrypted (Video Witness)
- Every seal is anchored on Hedera Hashgraph mainnet
- Every notarization auto-generates a SOC2-ready audit packet
- Counter-parties can verify your document via [Public Audit Trail](./public-audit-trail.md) without contacting us

**Is RON legal in my state?** RON is legally recognized in 44+ US states as of 2026. Our [Find Notaries](./find-notaries.md) page only shows notaries qualified to serve your state.

**How much does it cost?** Typical fees: $25–$50 per signature. Bulk pricing for closings.

**How long does a ceremony take?** 10–20 minutes for a single document. 30–45 minutes for closings.
