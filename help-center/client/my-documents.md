# My Documents

**One searchable inbox for every notarized document, Quick Seal, draft, and vault item you've ever created.**

## What it is
A unified document hub. Filterable by date, document type, notarization status, and blockchain seal status. Searchable by signer name, document title, or hash.

## How to use it
- **Search bar** — find any doc by name, signer, or content keyword
- **Filters** — limit to Sealed on Chain, Notarized, Drafts, or Vault
- **Bulk actions** — download, share verification links, archive

## Pro tip
Hit the **"Sealed on chain"** filter to see only blockchain-anchored documents. These are the ones you can share with public verification links — the verifier doesn't even need a NotaryChain account.

## FAQ
**Is there a storage limit?** Standard plan: 5 GB. Pro: unlimited.

**How long are documents retained?** Forever, unless you explicitly delete them. (And the blockchain proof persists even after deletion.)
