# Asset Vault

**The encrypted digital safe-deposit box for the documents your family will need when something goes wrong.**

## What it is
A AES-256-encrypted vault for the documents that *must* be findable in emergencies: wills, trusts, deeds, life insurance, beneficiary forms, crypto recovery phrases. Designate beneficiaries. When you pass, they receive a one-time access link.

## When to use it
- Storing your will, trust, healthcare directive, POA
- Storing property deeds and titles
- Crypto wallet recovery phrases (sealed)
- Inventories of online accounts, password manager backups
- Life insurance policies and beneficiary forms

## How to set it up in 4 steps
1. Open **Asset Vault** from `/dashboard`.
2. Upload your documents (encrypted on the way in).
3. Designate **beneficiaries** (name, email, relationship).
4. Optionally enable the **dead-man's switch** — if you don't check in for 90 days, beneficiaries are automatically notified.

## Pro tip
Add your **digital** assets, not just paper ones — crypto wallet recovery phrases (sealed), online account inventories, password manager backups. This is the #1 thing financial planners ask for and the #1 thing nobody actually does.

## FAQ
**Can beneficiaries see my documents now?** No. They only receive access after the trigger fires (your death certificate uploaded by an executor, or the dead-man's switch expires).

**Is the data on-chain?** Only the hash is anchored to Hedera for tamper-proof verification. The actual document stays encrypted in our vault.

**Can I revoke beneficiary access?** Yes — at any time, instantly.
