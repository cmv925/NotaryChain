# Doc Compare

**Upload v1 and v2 of any document. See exactly what changed — with AI explaining what each change means in practice.**

## What it is
A redlining tool that compares two versions side-by-side, highlighting additions in green, deletions in red, and changed financial figures or dates in amber. The AI explains each change in one sentence of plain English.

## When to use it
- "Minor revision" emails from your attorney or counter-party
- Lease renewals — comparing this year vs. last year
- Vendor contract renewals
- Tracking M&A or financing term-sheet evolutions
- Auditing template-versioned documents

## How to use it in 3 steps
1. Click **Doc Compare**.
2. Upload v1, then v2.
3. Review the highlighted diff and AI summary.

## Pro tip
When opposing counsel sends you a "minor revision," run it through Doc Compare before signing. "Minor" sometimes means "we slipped in a clause that changes the deal."

## FAQ
**Does it work on scanned PDFs?** Yes — OCR runs automatically on image-based PDFs.

**Limit on file size?** 100 MB per document.
