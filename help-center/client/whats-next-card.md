# What's Next? Card

**The one card at the top of your dashboard that tells you the single most important thing to do right now.**

## What it is
An AI-driven decision card. Instead of dumping a 12-item to-do list on you, it computes the *single* highest-priority next action across all your data — expired documents, pending notarizations, awaiting-action sessions, upcoming renewals — and shows it as a single colored card.

## What you'll see
- **Amber:** Something is expired or expiring soon — "Renew Power of Attorney"
- **Coral:** Something is waiting for you — "Continue where you left off"
- **Emerald:** Empty state — "You're all caught up"
- **Navy/neutral:** Routine reminder

## How to use it
Look at it. Click the button. Done.

## Pro tip
If the card says "You're all caught up," it really means it — go take the rest of the day off. Combine with [Asset Vault](./asset-vault.md) beneficiary settings and a 90-day dead-man's switch for true peace of mind.

## FAQ
**How often does it update?** Live — every time you reload, and continuously via WebSocket while you're on the page.

**Can I dismiss it?** Yes — once you click the CTA or resolve the underlying issue, it advances to your next priority.
