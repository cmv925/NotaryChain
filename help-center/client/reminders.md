# Reminders

**Calendar nudges for document expirations, renewal deadlines, and beneficiary check-ins.**

## What it is
A renewal-aware notification system. Wills should be reviewed every 3–5 years. POAs expire. Insurance beneficiaries need updates after life events. Reminders tracks all of it.

## How to set up reminders
1. When you upload a document, set a reminder ("Review POA in 24 months").
2. When the date arrives, you get an email *and* the **What's Next?** card lights up on your dashboard.

## Pro tip
Add a recurring **"annual estate review"** reminder for January 1st. 15 minutes of housekeeping per year prevents six-figure messes later.

## FAQ
**Can I share reminders with my attorney or family?** Yes — invite them as collaborators on specific documents.

**Does it sync with Google Calendar / iCal?** Export any reminder as an `.ics` file or subscribe to your full feed.
