## Slide 5 — The Solution: Three Bets

### Bet 1 — AI Forensics

GPT-5.2 Vision scans every document. Detects copy-paste seals, font mismatches, altered text, and collisions with prior seals on-chain.

### Bet 2 — Hedera Public Blockchain

Every seal → SHA-256 hash → submitted to Hedera Consensus Service on **mainnet**. Globally verifiable forever. Sub-cent per transaction.

### Bet 3 — Living Identity

Continuous biometric re-attestation. Identity drift triggers WebSocket alerts in real time.

📣 *"We make three architectural bets that together no incumbent has. AI forensics before the seal. Hedera blockchain during the seal. And — uniquely — continuous identity after the seal. Together: a continuous-trust notary system, not a one-shot stamp."*
