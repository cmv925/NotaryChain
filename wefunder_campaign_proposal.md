We believe in transparency. Here's what could go wrong:

**Regulatory risk.** Each state has different RON laws. We need state-by-state certification to operate legally. If regulations tighten or reverse, our addressable market shrinks.

**Competition.** Established players like Notarize and DocuSign have larger sales teams and brand recognition. They could add blockchain features (though none have in 5+ years).

**Technology risk.** Our blockchain partner (Hedera Hashgraph) is a third-party infrastructure. If Hedera experiences significant issues, our sealing feature would be temporarily unavailable (though the platform has graceful degradation).

**Revenue risk.** We are pre-revenue. Our projections are based on market analysis and comparable SaaS metrics, not historical data. Actual results may differ.

**Key person risk.** The platform was built by a small, high-velocity team. Loss of key team members could slow development.
